ABOUT US

SWASTIK Hospital group is a Multispecialty health care provider in the state of Odisha. SWASTIK Hospital is established with the sole mission and commit to provide affordable qualitative healthcare and adopting a service-oriented delivery model, SWASTIK hospital provides cost-effective medical care, with an compromising commitment to its core purpose “ to provide care that people trust”. SWASTIK hospital Cuttack was founded in 2015 under Swastik Charitable Trust 50 bedded Multi specialty and supper specialty hospital.

MISSION

QUALITY POLICY : "We at SWASTIK Hospital are committed to render quality health care with human touch at optimal cost by continually upgrading our skills and resources for better care of our patients and complying with applicable regulatory.The Hospital is also trying to achieve quality service as per NABH standard".

QUALITY OBJECTIVES:

  • To provide health care based on state of technology and expert at optimum cost ensuring value for money
  • To provide multispecialty Medicare under one roof.
  • To provide a delightful ambience and excellent medical care with human touch by integrating team work and effective quality system with monitoring and feedback.
